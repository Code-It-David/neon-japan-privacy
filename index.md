# Neon Japan Privacy Policy

**Effective date: July 2, 2026**

Neon Japan is a Japanese kana-learning application developed by Kami Light Studio.

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to:

**kamistudio.support@gmail.com**

## Summary

Neon Japan does not require an account.

The app has no advertisements and does not use analytics or tracking services.

## Data Collection and Sharing

Neon Japan itself does not collect, sell, or share personal or sensitive user data.

The app does not send your learning progress to Kami Light Studio or to a Kami Light Studio server.

## Learning Data

Your learning progress is stored on your device. This may include:

- completed lessons;
- practice results;
- streaks and daily goals;
- weak characters;
- learning preferences and settings.

This information is used only to provide the learning and progress features inside the app.

Core learning content is included with the application.

## Device Backups

Your Android device or Google account may back up or transfer application data depending on your device and account settings.

These backup and transfer features are controlled by Android or Google, not by Neon Japan. Restoration of learning progress is not guaranteed.

## Deleting Your Data

You can delete your learning progress from the Progress screen inside Neon Japan.

Clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app may also remove locally stored learning information.

## Contacting Us

If you contact Kami Light Studio by email, we receive the information you choose to include in your message. This information is used only to respond to your request.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the features or data-handling practices of Neon Japan change.

Any updated version will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
